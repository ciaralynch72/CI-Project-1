A community market for all

The Kilworth Farmers' Market provides local, sustainably produced fresh foods and products in ways that build our community, support local food growers and producers, and promote access to healthy food for everyone who lives in the locality.

Our Mission

Locally producd. Locally ran. Locally supported.


This market is for all of us to share what we created, purchase what we need and engage with family and friends and come to gether to support all our wonderful local suppliers.
