The Kilworth Farmers' Market wiil run from May 1 to October 31 hail rain or shine

Come join over 30 vendors and support your local economy. Find local produce, fresh baked goods, hand crafted items, flowers and everything in between.


We are proud to partner with vendors old and new to curate a community market that we hope will foster connection, friendship and a sense of community for all those that attend all while supporting our passionate vendors.

Our Mission

The Kilworth Farmers' Market provides local, sustainably produced fresh foods and products in ways that build our community, support local food growers and producers, and promote access to healthy food for everyone who lives in the locality.

Our Values

To champion and promote local farmers, crafts people, producers and makers within out community.
