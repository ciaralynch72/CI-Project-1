The Market Community

Local markets are crucial selling spaces for our vendors.
Direct sales provide a greater return for their labour-intensive efforts as they compete with an expanding industrial food system.

Markets also contribute positively to community health and the local economy.
They provide opportunities for social connection and for consumers to better understand where our food comes from.
